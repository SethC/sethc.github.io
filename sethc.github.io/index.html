#Why MPG predict?

Many people drive cars. Cars often requie gas. Gas costs money.

Wouldn't you want to know exactly how many miles per gallon your car should get - at least based on MPG predict?....

Or maybe you are purchasing a car and you need a fast and easy way to predict how many miles per gallon different cars may get.

#MPG Predict is quick and easy.

If you know the number of cylinders that your car has, or the car you are interested in purchasing has, then you can use MPG Predict.

Try it for yourself here.

#MPG Predict is very straight forward.

MPG predict uses the mtcars data set in R to tell you what the average MPG is for cars with the same cylinders as the car you are interested in.

First load required package dplyr and the dataset mtcars.

require(dplyr)

## Loading required package: dplyr

## Warning in library(package, lib.loc = lib.loc, character.only = TRUE,
## logical.return = TRUE, : there is no package called 'dplyr'

data(mtcars)

Then define the MPG Predict function:

avgMPG<-function(cylinders) mean(subset(mtcars, cyl == cylinders)$mpg)

#And finally....

here is an example of the MPG predict magic at work.

exampleCyl<-4

avgMPG(exampleCyl)

## [1] 26.66364

For more examples try MPG Predict live on shinapps.io at https://nstox.shinyapps.io/mpgPrediction
